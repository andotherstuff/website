How we work

Three hubs, one collective.

AOS works as a living ecosystem. Our work moves through three interconnected hubs—the Lab, the Studio, and the Foundry—so ideas can grow from early exploration to real-world products and long-term stewardship.

The Lab

The Lab is where ideas spark and take shape. It's a collaborative environment for creators, founders, activists, and researchers to explore problems, surface insights, and imagine what's possible—long before there's a roadmap.

In the Lab, we:
  • Host problem-framing and ideation sessions
  • Explore themes like trust & safety, identity, and web-of-trust
  • Prototype concepts that might stay exploratory—and that's okay
  • Connect people across disciplines to unlock new patterns

The Studio

The Studio is where ideas become real. It's the production and services hub that helps teams design, build, and launch meaningful freedom-tech projects.

Studio support includes:
  • Product, UX, research, design, and go-to-market guidance
  • Back-office services: accounting, finance, tax, legal, budgeting, planning
  • Software and hardware development
  • Infrastructure and security expertise grounded in open protocols

The Foundry

The Foundry is where ideas mature and find their long-term path. We don't run a fund—we help projects explore governance, sustainability, and community-aligned ways to grow.

In the Foundry, we:
  • Offer governance and organizational design guidance
  • Support teams preparing for early-stage funding or philanthropic partnerships
  • Explore sustainable business models for freedom tech
  • Make mission-aligned contributions that strengthen the commons
